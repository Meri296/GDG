Edge Computing
A distributed computing paradigm that brings computation and data storage closer to the source of data (such as IoT devices) to reduce latency and improve processing speed.
MQTT (Message Queuing Telemetry Transport)
A lightweight messaging protocol designed for low-bandwidth, high-latency, or unreliable networks, commonly used in IoT devices for efficient communication.
LoRaWAN (Long Range Wide Area Network)
A wireless communication protocol designed for long-range, low-power IoT applications, ideal for connecting battery-operated devices in smart cities, agriculture, and industrial automation.
